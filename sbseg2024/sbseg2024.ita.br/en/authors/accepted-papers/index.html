WFC Talks:

Title: Rapid Forensic Analysis for Incident Response and Material Screening

Speaker: Guilherme Martini Dalpian – Federal Criminal Expert – Federal Police

The aim of the presentation is to showcase a tool used by the Federal Police during Search and Seizure operations for locating evidence and screening materials. This tool is based on open-source code, utilizing a custom Linux distribution with tools developed internally by the Federal Police, such as the Digital Evidence Indexer and Processor (IPED) and the Digital Evidence Locator (LED). This tool has been successfully used to locate illicit files, such as in cases of child sexual abuse, ensuring a forensic approach and the preservation of the chain of custody.

Mini-bio: Guilherme is a Federal Criminal Expert at the Federal Police since 2005, he holds a degree in Computer Engineering from the Aeronautics Institute of Technology. He has experience in the field of Computer Science, with an emphasis on Forensic Computing and Information Security.
