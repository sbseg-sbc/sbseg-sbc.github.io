Registration

REGISTER FOR SBSEG 2014

For every article or paper accepted for publication at SBSeg 2014, at least one of the authors is required to register for the symposium at the Professional level by October 11, 2014.

WTICG: for authors of papers accepted for the WTICG workshop, an author registering at the Student level by October 11, 2014 is sufficient.

The following is a price chart for the basic registration schemes for SBSeg 2014:

Basic Symposium Registration Until Sept 9th Until Oct 11th After Oct 11th
Student member of SBC R$ 35,00 R$ 40,00 R$ 80,00
Student non-member of SBC R$ 40,00 R$ 45,00 R$ 100,00
Professional member of SBC R$ 220,00 R$ 270,00 R$ 320,00
Professional non-member of SBC R$ 300,00 R$ 350,00 R$ 400,00

Basic registration entitles symposium attendees to participate in the technical sessions, lectures, tutorials, panel discussions and workshops. Additionally, symposium attendees will receive the following materials: conference folder and a digital copy of the conference proceedings; furthermore attendees are given full access to the coffee breaks and the opening cocktail reception.

Tutorials Registration (Individually Priced) Until Sept 11th Until Oct 11th After Oct 11th
Student member of SBC R$ 20,00 R$ 25,00 R$ 35,00
Student non-member of SBC R$ 30,00 R$ 35,00 R$ 45,00
Professional member of SBC R$ 55,00 R$ 70,00 R$ 95,00
Professional non-member of SBC R$ 75,00 R$ 90,00 R$ 115,00

In order to register for a Tutorials, an attendee must also register for the SBSeg conference at the basic registration level.

Discounts

Tutorials: Conference participants purchasing registration for one tutorial will receive a 50% discount on a second tutorials registration. If a participant registers for two tutorials they will receive a free registration credit for a third tutorials.

Registration Payment for SBSeg 2014

Registration payment can be made by sending a Registration Payment Slip, by Credit Card, by Direct Deposit via Banco do Brazil, Requesting an Invoice, or submitting Declaration of Intent to Attend.
