Getting Here

Conference Room

SBSeg will take place in the Centro de Atividades Didáticas de Ciências Humanas (CAD 2), which is exhibited in the following map www.ufmg.br/conheca/mapas. From there, you can go on foot to a) Restaurante Setorial I, a student restaurant; b) ICEX building (point 7, in the map), where our Computer Science Dept. is located; and c) Praça de Serviços (point 8, in the map), which is a sort of mall that embrances restaurantes, banks e other stores.

Transportation

Executive Airport Bus - Conexão Aeroporto

Conexão Aeroporto is a direct and main transit link between downtown Belo Horizonte and Tancredo Neves International Airport (CONFINS). A one way trip on Conexão Aeroporto costs R$ 21.00 and includes complementary Wifi. For more information on the Conexão Aeroporto service (schedule, pick-up and drop-off locations, etc.) please visit: www.conexaoaeroporto.com.br/home.
