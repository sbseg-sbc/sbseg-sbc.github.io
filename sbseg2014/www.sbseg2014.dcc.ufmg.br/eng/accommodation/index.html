Accommodation

UFMG is located in the Pampulha neighborhood, close to the Mineirão Stadium, and within proximity of many hotel options. Below is a list of some of the hotel options in the Pampulha neighborhood. Another suggested neighborhood with many hotel options is the Savassi neighborhood, famous for its restaurants, bars, and entertainment options. Savassi is linked to UFMG’s campus by various bus lines and connections to the Bus Rapid Transit System - MOVE.

San Diego Suítes Pampulha – Event Main Hotel

San Diego Suites Pampulha is a modern and elegant hotel situated by Pampulha Lake, one of the most important cultural heritage regions of the city. The hotel includes 170 comfortably furnished suites to provide maximum comfort for guests. The hotel is located in close proximity to Belo Horizonte’s Pampulha Domestic Airport and 35 km from Confins International Airport. San Diego Suites’s location is also close to one of the main transit corridors of BH allowing for easy access to all regions of the city.
Location: Pampulha

Pampulha Open Hotel - Deluxe Hostel

Offering a restaurant, Pampulha Open Hotel - Deluxe Hostel is located in front of Pampulha Lake in Belo Horizonte. Free WiFi access is available and a daily breakfast is included. The hostel offers three dormitory categories. Each features a panoramic glass view and includes a shared bathroom. Extras include bed linens and towels. At Pampulha Open Hotel - Deluxe Hostel there is a terrace with a view. Other facilities offered at the property include a shared lounge.
Location: Pampulha

Booking Special Conditions

Bristol Pampulha Lieu

Excellent location and quality of service define and mark the Bristol Pampulha Lieu. Located in the heart of BH’s Pampulha neighborhood, the hotel is a short distance from the Pampulha Domestic Airport, UFMG, the Minerião Stadium and Mineirinho Arena. The Bristol Pampulha Lieu offers guests quality service, guaranteed by ISO 9001 certification, along with stunning views of Belo Horizonte.
Location: Pampulha

Bristol Jaraguá Hotel

With an excellent location, the Bristol Jaraguá Hotel, one of the newest members of the hotel chain, opened its doors in 2012. The hotel is located near Avenue Antônio Carlos (linking Pampulha with downtown Belo Horizonte), Pampulha Domestic Airport, UFMG, the Mineirão Stadium and Mineirinho Arena, and the Beltway (Anel Rodoviário). Along with the well designed suites, the hotel’s contemporary structure includes: a ballroom, women’s floor, recreation area, and a restaurant with a balcony, ensuring comfort, safety and convenience for guests.
Location: Pampulha

Quality Hotel Pampulha

Located on one of Belo Horizonte’s main avenues (Avenue Antonio Carlos), and near UFMG, Pampulha Domestic Airport, and near major tourist attractions, Quality Hotel Pampulha features 176 suites which include: soundproofing, AC and heating, 32” LCD TV, electronic safe, hair dryer, Wi-Fi access in three categories: Superior, Deluxe, and Business Class.
Location: Pampulha

Hotel Ibis Liberdade

Located in a desirable district of Belo Horizonte, close to the major events venues (Minas Centro and Expominas), the ibis BH Liberdade hotel has 130 extremely comfortable apartments, all with double beds.
