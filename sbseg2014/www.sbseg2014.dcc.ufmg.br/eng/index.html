The Brazilian Symposium on Information and Computational Systems Security (SBSeg) is an annual symposium sponsored by the Brazilian Society of Computer Science (SBC). SBSeg is Brazil’s primary venue for informatics and computational security researchers and professionals to present research results, exchange ideas, debate, and partake in other relevant activities.

The 14th annual symposium will take place from November 3rd to 6th in the Social Sciences' Teaching Activities Center at the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG) in the Pampulha region of Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais. The coordination of this year’s symposium falls under the direction of UFMG’s Department of Computer Science. The symposium will include technical sessions, short courses, lectures, workshops, and presentations from internationally renowned guest speakers.
