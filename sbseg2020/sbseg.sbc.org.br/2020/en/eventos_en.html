Event


The Brazilian Symposium on Information and Computational Systems Security (SBSeg), held annually since 2001 by Brazilian Computer Society (SBC), is the leading Brazilian academic event in the area of ​​security. The SBSeg involves about 300 participants, including professors, researchers, graduate students, and specialist professionals from Brazil and abroad, who present and discuss the latest scientific and technological advances in information security and computer systems. SBSeg enjoys an excellent reputation due to the seriousness of its organization, the diversity and breadth of its proposed activities, the timeliness of the topics covered and the rigorous selection process.

SBSeg 2020 will provide a rich variety of activities such as:

  • Capture the Flag (CTF)
  • Short Courses
  • XIV Workshop of Scientific initiation and Graduation Works (WTICG)
  • Testing and Dissertation Competition on Information Security and Computer Systems (CTDSeg)
  • IV Tool Session
  • X Digital Identity Management Workshop (WGID)
  • IX Computer Forensic Workshop (WFG)
  • Coordination of the Corporate Security Forum (FSC)
