Generall Call

The twentieth edition of the Brazilian Symposium on Information Security and Computer Systems (SBSeg) will take place from October 13 to 16, 2020 and is held by the Brazilian Computer Society (SBC) and organized by the National Laboratory for Scientific Computing (LNCC), by National Institute of Metrology, Quality and Technology (Inmetro) and the Institute of Computing (IC) of the Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF). In view of the risk scenario associated with the Covid-19 pandemic, the organization of SBSeg 2020, in agreement with CE-Seg, chose to hold the event in the online model. Even so, all measures will be taken to ensure maximum interaction between the participants and the continuous strengthening of the Brazilian community of information security and computer systems. According to current CAPES criteria, the articles presented and published in the annals of SBSeg 2020 are classified in the restricted index (Qualis A4).

Important Dates:

  • Article registration deadline: July 10, 2020 July 31, 2020

  • Deadline for submission of articles: July 17, 2020 August 05, 2020

  • Result Notification: September 11, 2020

  • Final version and entries: 13 to 16 October 2020
