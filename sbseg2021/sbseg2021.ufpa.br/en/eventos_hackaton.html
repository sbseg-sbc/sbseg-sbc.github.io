II Hackathon - HackSBseg

The Brazilian Symposium on Information and Computational Systems Security (SBSeg) will have a hackathon for the second time. The goal of the HackSBseg is to stimulate the development of practical projects within information security, so that area enthusiasts meet for lightning-fast team-based project development - and have fun in the process.
