Official dinner (by adhesion)

The Official Dinner of the event will be held on 24/10/2018, starting at 7:30pm at the restaurant Camarões Restaurante (Av. Engenheiro Roberto Freire, 3980 - Ponta Negra, Natal - RN, 59090-000). The price per person is R$90.00. This value includes a special menu (2 options of starters, 3 options of main course and 2 options of dessert), soft drinks, fruit juice, mineral water, coconut water and cafe espresso. This value does not include alcoholic drinks that can be consumed, being charged individually. The purchase of the entrance must be made EXCLUSIVELY by the https://centraldesistemas.sbc.org.br/ecos until the day 17/10/2018. No dinner tickets will be sold during the event!
