                             CALL FOR PAPERS
                WORKSHOP ON DIGITAL IDENTITY MANAGEMENT


           22nd November 2012, Curitiba – Paraná - Brazil
                - Co-located with the XII SBSeg 2012- 

 
Identity management (IdM) is an integrated system of policies, business processes 
and technologies that enables organizations to deal with the identities of its 
users. Identity management also involves issues related to certification and 
management of the lifecycle of digital identities, together with the legal aspects.

Identity management is an active research topic and is tending to be for many 
years. This tendency is because of many different issues to be considered, such 
as: the ease of use of systems, user privacy, strong security, single sign on, 
total cost of ownership, user profiling and retention of users by service providers,
 scalability, fine grained access control, personalization of services and 
interoperability.

The Second Workshop on Identity Management (WGID) aims to be a forum for discussions
 and technical presentations of recent works and / or in progress about the state 
of art in the identity management. In addition, we seek to also identify the challenges
 of the research in this area and enable the mapping of research groups.

INSTRUCTIONS TO AUTHORS
Submitted papers should describe relevant research, experiment reports, case studies and 
work in progress. Articles can be submitted in Portuguese or English, only in PDF format.
 The text should be formatted using the SBC template. Papers have a limit of 10 pages,
 including figures, tables, references and appendix.

Papers will undergo a review process by the Program Committee. Accepted papers must be 
presented by one of the registered authors in order to be published in the Proceedings 
of the 12th SBSeg.
